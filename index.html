Well well well, what do we have here?

So I started making this web-page or a blog with the thought in mind that I would like to share and store what I learn while working on the projects related to my graduate studies and several personal projects as I tinker around with things, so that someone else who might be looking so solve the same problems or somewhat related problems could find the information at one place. And also so that if I my self have to do some thing again then these articles here would serve as my notes.

At the moment the web page has two sections. I update it periodically or when I think that I should make notes of something that I develop or a new thing that I might have learnt along the way.

The "Blog" section is where I would be sharing my thoughts and just ramble about things that might be on my mind or that I might find interesting in the moment. I can already foresee myself updating this section less frequently, but its still nice to have.

One of my favorite pastimes is painting and sketching so I plan to add a section(Art-Gallery) where I can show-case my art. I will get around to it soon, but I thought that I will add this point here so it will act as a reminder to me. Anyhow, feel free to peruse the articles I post here and If you have any feedback do let me know. :) Ahmad.
